About Room 222

COME JOIN US AT THE BRICK

Conveniently located at 222 E. Sheridan st. Room 222 provides a unique experience unlike anything else in the nightlife hub of historic Bricktown.
Presented as an upscale Lounge and Restaurant, Room 222 offers hand crafted cocktails, premium beers, and an extensive wine collection accompanied by live bands and our house chef's cooking made with affection and generosity. The staff provides an effortless charm which gives even the most conservative guests a feeling warmer than the inside of a granny's apple pie.
A must see for locals and guests alike Room 222 at the Brick is a premier destination for entertainment in Oklahoma City
